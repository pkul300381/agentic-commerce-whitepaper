# Term Paper Proposal

## Working title
**A Mathematical Model for Multi-Agent AI Systems Using Game Theory**

## Problem statement
Modern digital commerce is increasingly mediated by autonomous AI agents that negotiate and transact with limited human intervention. Existing frameworks under-specify strategic behavior among heterogeneous participants.

## Proposed contribution
Develop a formal game-theoretic and optimization-based model for merchant, user, and bank agents in tokenized commerce settings, and evaluate equilibrium and welfare outcomes under alternative mechanism designs.

## Methodology
- Formal model specification (players, strategies, utilities, constraints)
- Equilibrium analysis
- Comparative statics and scenario simulation
- Policy and governance implications
